# Exercise 3: The end-to-end flow

> **Objective:** Put everything together! Use the Voice AI assistant to create an incident, assign it to the L1 Service Desk AI Specialist, and watch the full Zero Touch Support pipeline — from voice call through autonomous resolution — in action.
>
> ⏱️ **Total time:** ~15 minutes

***

### ❇️ Step 1 — Impersonate Ravi Kapoor

1. From the icon on top right, pull down and choose impersonate&#x20;
2.  Choose **Ravi Kapoor**
3.  Select **Impersonate user** to confirm.

    <figure><img src=".gitbook/assets/2026-06-24 10.21.51.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>



***

### ❇️ Step 2 — Assign the Incident to the L1 Service Desk AI Specialist

1. As Ravi, on the Service Operations Workspace, select the **List** button on the far left, then under **Incidents** select **Assigned to you**. Find the incident pre-seeded for this lab by its short description — **"ZScaler tunnel dropping — cannot reach internal apps"** (`INC0010015` on this instance, though the exact number may differ on yours).

    > **Note:** If this incident isn't in **New** state, update its state to **New** before continuing so the assignment step below triggers correctly.



<figure><img src=".gitbook/assets/2026-06-24 12.01.06 (3).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>



2. On the incident record, navigate to the **Details** tab.
3. Scroll down to the **Assignment** section.
4. In the **Assigned to** field, type `ai` and select the AI Specialist you named in Exercise 1 (e.g., **Athena Service Desk AI Specialist** — your first name, plus the fixed last name "Service Desk AI Specialist") from the dropdown.
5. An **Assign** dialog will appear with the following details:
   * **Assigned to:** L1 Service Desk AI Specialist
   * **Work notes:** A Now Assist-generated summary of the issue (e.g., _"Issue: A user is experiencing an inability to access the internet on their laptop..."_). Don't forget to select the **Now Assist sparkle icon** if the summary isn't already generated — it doesn't always populate automatically.
   * A note indicating the work notes were generated by Now Assist — review before saving.
6.  Select **Save** in the dialog to confirm the assignment.

    > Once saved, the incident state will change to **In Progress** and the L1 Service Desk AI Specialist will begin working the incident autonomously.

### ❇️ Step 3 — Watch the Agentic Process in Action

1. On the right sidebar of the incident, locate and select the **Agentic Processes** menu item (the robot/agent icon).
2. You'll see the **Zero Touch Service Desk Agent** panel showing:
   * **Status:** In progress
   * **Owner:** L1 Service Desk AI Specialist
   * **Started at:** The timestamp when the process began
3. Select **Show steps** to expand the full step-by-step agentic process.
4.  Observe the AI Specialist working through each stage:

    * ✅ Started AI Agent "Zero Touch Service..."
    * ✅ Fetching details of the given task
    * ✅ Task details fetched
    * ✅ Checked on remaining steps
    * ✅ Solution research complete
    * ✅ Data sources fetched
    * ✅ Finding potential solutions
    * ✅ Solutions fetched
    * ✅ Non-customer actions filtered
    * ✅ Executing the determined resolution...
    * 🔴 Started AI Agent "Zero Touch Service..." _(DEX remediation triggered)_

    > Watch this in real time — the AI Specialist is classifying the incident, researching knowledge sources, identifying potential solutions, and executing the resolution autonomously. When a device-level fix is needed, it triggers the DEX remediation agent to act directly on the end-user's device.

    > **Be patient:** this takes about 8-10 minutes. It's normal to see "Checked on remaining steps" appear several times while it works.

### ❇️ Step 4 — Review the Resolution

1. In the incident **Activity** feed (center panel), review the work notes posted by the L1 Service Desk AI Specialist:
   * **\[Now Assist generated]** — Recommended incident field updates (e.g., Service: SAP Enterprise Services)
   * **Issue summary** — A detailed description of the issue and context
   * **Resolution notes** — The solution identified and actions taken
2.  Confirm the incident fields have been updated by the AI Specialist (Category, Service, State, etc.).

    > 🎉 **Congratulations!** You've just completed a full Zero Touch Support flow — from an employee's voice call, through AI-powered triage and investigation, to autonomous resolution and device remediation. No human agent touched this incident.

***

### Now go through and assign the remaining Incidents

**You can now go through each of the remaining incidents assigned to Ravi and switch them to the L1 Specialist. Most will be resolved autonomously — but at least one will fall back, with the L1 Specialist reassigning it to the IT Support group. That's expected: it's the escalation behavior configured in Exercise 1.**
